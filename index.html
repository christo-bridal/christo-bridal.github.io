Your Dream Event, Our Passion

Welcome to Christo Bridal Accessories & Event Planning - where dreams come alive! As a young, passionate event planner based in the heart of Ankpa, Kogi State, I bring fresh creativity, boundless energy, and unwavering dedication to every celebration I touch.

Starting my journey in the beautiful world of event planning, I've discovered that every love story deserves to be celebrated in extraordinary ways. Specializing in wedding events and joyful celebrations, I pour my heart into crafting experiences that reflect your unique personality and vision.

From intimate ceremonies to grand celebrations, I handle every detail with meticulous care - from stunning bridal accessories that make you shine, to seamless event coordination that lets you enjoy every precious moment. My mission is simple: to create magical memories that last a lifetime.
