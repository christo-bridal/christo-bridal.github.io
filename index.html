About Christo Bridal Accessories & Event Planning

Christo Bridal Accessories and Event Planning is a full-service event consultancy renowned for its commitment to excellence, precision, and style. With a foundation built on project management principles and creative artistry, we specialize in bridal styling, accessories curation, and comprehensive event execution. Our clientele spans private individuals, brands, and corporate institutions seeking professional planning and design services across Nigeria and beyond.

Our goal is to create unforgettable experiences tailored to your personality and brand essence. Whether it is an intimate wedding, a grand celebration, or a corporate function, we bring vision to reality with elegance and efficiency.
